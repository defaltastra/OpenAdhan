OpenAdhan is a prayer times app with complete privacy protection. No tracking, no ads, no user accounts required.

Features:
• Accurate prayer times based on astronomical calculations
• 15+ calculation methods (MWL, ISNA, Makkah, and more)
• Customizable notifications for each prayer
• Save multiple prayer locations
• Upload custom adhan (call to prayer) audio or use defaults
• Dark and light themes
• Multilingual support (English, Arabic, French)
• Home screen widgets for quick prayer time access

All data stored locally on your device. No cloud sync, no analytics, no user data collection.
