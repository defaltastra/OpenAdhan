OpenAdhan is a prayer times app with complete privacy protection. No tracking, no ads, no user accounts required.

## Features
- Accurate prayer times based on astronomical calculations
- 15+ calculation methods (MWL, ISNA, Makkah, and more)
- Customizable notifications for each prayer
- Save multiple prayer locations
- Upload custom adhan (call to prayer) audio or use defaults
- Dark and light themes
- Multilingual support (English, Arabic, French)
- Home screen widgets for quick prayer time access

## Privacy First
- All data stored locally on your device
- No internet connection required after initial setup
- No tracking or analytics
- No user accounts
- No ads whatsoever

## How to Use
1. **Set Location**: Select your country and city to get accurate prayer times
2. **Choose Calculation Method**: Pick from 15+ methods to match your local mosque's calculations
3. **Configure Notifications**: Enable/disable adhan alerts for each prayer
4. **Customize Adhan**: Choose from default audio files or upload your own
5. **View Qibla**: Use the built-in compass to find the direction towards Mecca

All settings and data are saved automatically on your device.
